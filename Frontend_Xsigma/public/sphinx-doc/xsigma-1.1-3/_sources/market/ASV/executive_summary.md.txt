## Limitations of ASV

### Model Constraints

- **Limited Flexibility**: Minimal parameters restrict fine-tuning capabilities compared to higher-order models
- **Wing Challenges**: High wing volatilities can create numerical convergence issues in Monte Carlo simulations
- **Arbitrage Constraints**: Requires external framework for butterfly arbitrage prevention
- **Parameter Bounds**: Limited ability to capture extreme market regimes

### Operational Considerations

- **Calibration Complexity**: Requires sophisticated optimization for parameter fitting
- **Market Data Dependency**: Performance depends on quality and completeness of input data
- **Numerical Stability**: High volatility regimes may require special handling
- **Model Risk**: Simplified parameterization may not capture all market nuances
